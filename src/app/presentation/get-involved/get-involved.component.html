There are too many ways to get involved in App Outlet project and help us to build the software that you use and love.

If something is wrong when you is using App Outlet you can report it to us on our Github repository.

Feel free to report bugs, instabilities, spelling errors or even make suggestions. We carefully evaluate each one of them.

We love interaction. Please follow us on Twitter to get some news about us. Send a message on Gitter or on Telegram if you prefer.
